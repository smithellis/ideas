You Have An Idea.

For several days a week, you work in your unit/department/lab/area. No one knows your job or workplace better than you do. It's very probable that you have some ideas about how to make your job or department better. Whether it's a process improvement or technology development, we want your ideas! Don't be shy - an idea unshared is an idea unrealized.

If you choose not to give us your email or your name, we still want your idea. If you are nervous about sharing, then do it anonymously - but still share! Nothing improves if no one is working on it.

Ready? Give us your idea!

This is a tool for constructive ideas, not a place to complain. In other words, be sure you are submitting a change, suggestion or innovation - not just complaining about the lack of one. If you have an issue with an individual, or something going on that warrants immediate intervention for your safety or the safety of others - this isn't the right resource. Please contact HR or your immediate supervisor if you have those types of concerns. Those problems shouldn't be ignored!
